# App Store & Play Store Metadata

---

## iOS (App Store Connect)

**App Name:** CrewSync
**Subtitle:** Train. Race. Recruit.

**Description:**
CrewSync is the all-in-one rowing performance platform built for serious athletes and coaches. Connect your Concept2 erg, get AI-powered training plans, race head-to-head against anyone in the world, and build your recruiting profile — all in one place.

Train smarter: AI-generated training plans built from your actual erg data. Plans auto-adjust as you improve.

Track everything: Connect your Concept2 logbook and your full workout history syncs automatically. Live PM5 Bluetooth tracking with real-time split, stroke rate, watts, and force curves.

Race anyone: Head-to-head erg racing against teammates or strangers anywhere in the world. Live lane-by-lane view with real-time splits.

Get recruited: Build a public recruiting profile with your erg scores, improvement trends, and combine score. College coaches browse and contact athletes directly.

Recovery intelligence: Connect Whoop for strain tracking. Log sleep, weight, hydration, and nutrition. AI weekly insight summaries tell you exactly why your performance is changing.

For coaches: Full team management with drag-and-drop lineups, seat racing analysis, race optimizer, training plan generator, recruiting tools, and a Coaches Hub to discover and track recruits.
