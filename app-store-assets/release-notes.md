## Version 1.0.0 — Initial Release

Welcome to CrewSync — the all-in-one rowing performance platform for athletes, coaches, and recruits.

**What's included in 1.0:**

- Full Concept2 logbook sync — import your complete workout history automatically
- Live PM5 Bluetooth tracking — real-time split, stroke rate, watts, and force curves during every piece
- AI training plans and performance analytics — plans built from your erg data that adapt as you improve
- Head-to-head erg racing — race teammates or strangers worldwide with a live lane-by-lane view
- Recruiting profiles and Coaches Hub — athletes build public profiles; coaches browse and contact recruits directly
- Team management and lineup optimization — drag-and-drop lineups, seat racing analysis, and race optimizer
- Whoop integration — strain, recovery, and sleep data with AI weekly insight summaries
- Global verified leaderboard — Concept2-verified rankings across all weight classes and distances
- Calculators — AI 2k predictor, split calculator, watts converter, and pace tables
